    Our Story

    Baked from Passion, Swirled with Love

    Swirl & Bliss started in 2022 as a home kitchen experiment — one baker, one oven, and an obsession with the perfect cinnamon roll. What began as weekend bakes for friends quickly became Abuja's most talked-about sweet treat.

    We believe a great cinnamon roll should be cloud-soft, generously filled, and indulgently topped. Every flavour we create is tested dozens of times before it earns a spot on our menu.

    Today we bake fresh every morning, deliver city-wide, and serve hundreds of happy customers each week — but our kitchen philosophy hasn't changed: make every swirl count.
